FAQ

Does the app need internet?
No, it works fully offline.

Is my data stored in the cloud?
No, all data stays on your device.

Why don't I get daily notifications?
Enable notifications from the app settings and grant permission in system settings.

How do I change the notification time?
Inside the app: Settings → Daily Notification Time.
